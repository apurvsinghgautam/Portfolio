Bio

I am a Mater's student in Cybersecurity at GeorgiaTech, starting Fall'19. My research interests lie in Threat Intelligence and Network Security.

Previously, I worked as a Security Research Intern at Volon Cyber Security Pvt. Ltd. under Rahul Binjve, focusing on Threat Intelligence, gathering information on threats and designing data collect systems for the same.

During my undergrad at Symbiosis Institute of Technology, I worked on a research project Hacker Forum Exploit and Classification for Proactive Cyber Threat Intelligence that utilizes hacker forum data for proactive cyber threat intelligence. The results from this research shows that among all the models, deep learning model RNN GRU gives the best classification results with 99.025% accuracy and 96.56% precision.

I also wrote a research chapter with Prof. Pooja Kamat titled Recent Trends in the Era of Cybercrime and the Measures to Control Them which got published as Chapter 10 in the book Handbook of e-Business Security by CRC Press (Taylor and Francis Group).

I play CTFs in my free time and am learning many new things in cyber security domain everyday.
