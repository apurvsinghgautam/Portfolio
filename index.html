Bio

I'm a Master's student in Cybersecurity at GeorgiaTech, working with Prof. Paul Pearce. My research interests lie in Threat Intelligence and Network Security.

I am currently working as a Graduate Teaching Assistant at Georgia Institute of Technology for CS 4235/6035 Intro to Information Security course. I am also volunteering as a Teaching Assistant at Cybrary. I previously worked as a Graduate Teaching Assistant for OMSCS 6250 Computer Networks course.

During my first semester at GeorgiaTech, I worked with Prof. Maria Konte and Gaurav Pande on a research project that utilizes several threat intelligence feeds to assess network reputation and detecting & mitigating Volumetric anomalies using Machine Learning analysis using Python. It utilizes PARAFAC tensor decomposition to decompose higher-order tensors in order-1 tensors.

Previously, I worked as a Security Research Intern at Volon Cyber Security Pvt. Ltd. with Rahul Binjve, focusing on Threat Intelligence, gathering information on dark web threat data and designing data collect systems for the same.

During my undergrad at Symbiosis Institute of Technology, I worked on a research project with Prof. Pooja Kamat and Yamini Gahlot titled Hacker Forum Exploit and Classification for Proactive Cyber Threat Intelligence that utilizes hacker forum data for proactive cyber threat intelligence. The results from this research shows that among all the models that we used, deep learning model RNN GRU gives the best classification results with 99.025% accuracy and 96.56% precision. I further wrote a research paper that was presented at ICICT Conference proceedings hosted by RVS Technical Campus, Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, India and got published in Springer Lecture Notes in Networks and Systems, Volume 98.

I also wrote a research chapter with Prof. Pooja Kamat titled Recent Trends in the Era of Cybercrime and the Measures to Control Them which got published as Chapter 10 in the book Handbook of e-Business Security by CRC Press (Taylor and Francis Group).

I play CTFs in my free time and am learning many new things in cyber security domain everyday. I am really passionate about giving back to the community and helping others get into this field.
